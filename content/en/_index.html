Architecture & Integration

Where Privateer Fits

In a modern GRC Engineering workflow, there is often a "last mile" gap between policy definition and technical verification. Privateer functions at the Evaluation layer of automated governance — the engine that executes high-level controls against live infrastructure to produce verifiable, machine-readable evidence.

Instead of treating compliance as a manual reporting exercise, Privateer treats it as a data engineering problem.

Automated Control Mapping

Privateer is designed to ingest established control catalogs and translate them into executable tests. This allows organizations to automate their adherence to industry-standard baselines without building custom tooling from scratch.

  • FINOS Common Cloud Controls — Privateer can automatically validate that cloud configurations meet the specific compliance requirements of the financial services sector.
  • OpenSSF OSPS Baseline — For projects prioritizing supply chain security, Privateer runs checks against the OpenSSF's security benchmarks to ensure best practices are baked into the infrastructure.

Engineering-First Governance

By producing standardized outputs, Privateer eliminates the need for "human-in-the-loop" translation during an audit.

  • Execution — Privateer runs validation plugins directly against your environment.
  • Standardization — Results are emitted in a schema-agnostic format that downstream audit and reporting tools can consume immediately.
  • Interoperability — Privateer integrates with wider automated governance frameworks like Gemara, acting as a modular component in your existing CI/CD or monitoring stack.
