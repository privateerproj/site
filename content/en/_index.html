Architecture & Integration

Where Privateer Fits

Privateer bridges the gap between policy definition and technical verification, treating compliance as a data engineering problem rather than a manual reporting exercise.

Automated Control Mapping

Translate industry-standard control catalogs, such as the FINOS Common Cloud Controls and the OpenSSF OSPS Baseline, directly into executable validation tests.

Native Gemara Integration

Privateer operates natively at the Evaluation layer of automated governance. You can automatically generate executable plugins directly from Gemara Control Catalogs, and emit your results as standardized Gemara Evaluation Logs. (Learn more about the GRC engineering model at gemara.openssf.org).

Audit-Ready Execution

Run validation plugins against live infrastructure to produce machine-readable, schema-agnostic evidence that your downstream CI/CD, reporting, and monitoring tools can consume immediately.
